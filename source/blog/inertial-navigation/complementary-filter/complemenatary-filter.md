Choosing $\alpha$?

$$
\alpha = \tau / (\tau + dt) \
dt = 1/f_s
$$

where tau is the desired time constant (how fast you want the readings to respond) 
and $f_s$ is your sampling frequency. This equation is derived from filter/control theory
